## 🖌 Overview  
Tap Forms Advanced is a flexible personal database application that lets you design custom forms for organizing any type of structured data — contacts, collections, recipes, projects, inventory, and anything else you want to track with precision and ease.

Form design is visual and intuitive. Add fields of any supported type — text, number, date, photo, checkbox, rating, relationship, and more — and arrange them in a layout that matches the nature of the data you are capturing.

Relationship fields link records across different forms, enabling a connected data model that reflects real-world relationships between entities. Link an invoice to a client, a book to an author, or a project to a set of tasks.

Powerful search and filtering surface any record in seconds regardless of how large the database grows. Save common searches as named filters for instant access to frequently needed subsets of your data.

For organized individuals, researchers, and small businesses who need a personal database that adapts to their specific data rather than forcing data into a predefined structure, Tap Forms Advanced is the record management tool that works the way they think.

## 🚀 Why Choose Tap Forms Advanced?

Tap Forms Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Tap Forms Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
